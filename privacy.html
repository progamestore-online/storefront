Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. Here is exactly what we collect: nothing.

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Overview

ProGameStore and all applications hosted on the progamestore.online domain are designed with a strict no-data-collection policy. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or behavioral data of any kind.

Data Collection

We collect no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No IP address logging
  • No location data
  • No search history or browsing patterns

Cookies and Local Storage

Our applications do not use cookies of any kind — no session cookies, no tracking cookies, no third-party cookies. Applications may use your browser's local storage (localStorage or IndexedDB) solely to save your in-app progress and preferences on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with, embed, or load resources from any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no third-party scripts, pixels, beacons, or SDKs in our applications. Specifically:

  • No Google Analytics or similar analytics platforms
  • No advertising networks or ad trackers
  • No social media tracking pixels
  • No customer data platforms
  • No A/B testing or experimentation platforms

Data Sharing

Since we collect no data, we have no data to share. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose any information to third parties because no such information exists in our systems.

Accounts

ProGameStore applications do not require user accounts. There is no registration, no login, and no authentication. You use the apps anonymously by default.

Data Storage

All application data (progress, preferences, saved content) is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your device. This data is under your complete control. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings. We have no access to this data and no ability to retrieve it.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data from any user, we inherently comply with all children's data protection requirements. Our apps are safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, it will only become more protective of your privacy, never less. Our commitment to zero data collection is foundational to this platform.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our GitHub organization.
