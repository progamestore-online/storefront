Multiplayer infrastructure

Real-time WebSocket sessions backed by Cloudflare Durable Objects. Server-authoritative state — every move is validated by the server, so client-side cheats don't propagate. Sessions are private to the two players (and any spectators) and don't share state with other rooms.

Privacy

Account info, save state, and match history live on the platform. No third-party analytics, no ad pixels. Cancel your subscription and your data is deleted within 30 days unless you ask us to keep it (you can; we don't push).
